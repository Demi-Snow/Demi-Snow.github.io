Profile

For nine-plus years I've turned scattered, mistrusted data into trusted insight, scalable reporting, and the foundation leaders rely on to make confident decisions.

Day to day, that mostly looks like executive Power BI dashboards — built on solid DAX, star-schema models, and time-intelligence — the kind leadership checks every morning to see revenue, appointments, and program performance in real time. When the reporting needs it, I also build the SQL and Python underneath so the numbers in the dashboard are ones people can actually trust.

I'm equally at home as the analyst going deep on a single report or leading the team that builds a dozen of them — I've also stood up analytics functions from scratch, hiring and mentoring the analysts on them. My $5M+ in funding-related analysis has come from both kinds of work.

Two master's degrees shape how I work: a Master of Science in Data Science for the technical problem, and a Master of Public Administration for the organizational one. I read both — which is usually where the real answer lives.
